Race conditions demo

Tap a race to see the wind, water and weather at the moment it was rowed - including whether the breeze was a head, tail or cross wind down the course. Conditions are the hidden variable behind almost every fast or slow time.

Demo build. Weather is fetched live from the free Open-Meteo historical archive using each course's coordinates and the race time. Course bearings below are rough placeholders - send me the exact orientation of each course and I'll lock them in. If the fetch fails (offline), it falls back to illustrative sample data.
